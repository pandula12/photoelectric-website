About Us

At Photoelectric Energy, we are committed to driving the future of sustainable energy solutions in Sri Lanka. Our mission is to create a greener, more sustainable future for all.

Our Expertise

With over 25 years of experience in the Chinese market through our parent company, Foshan Sky Ceramics, we bring extensive expertise to the solar market in Sri Lanka. We import high-quality solar panels from JA Solar and inverters from DEYE, ensuring competitive prices and global standards.
